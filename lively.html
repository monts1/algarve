Saturday's lively night.

A marina full of options. Start at Atlantic Bar, see how the night goes - bar-hop, escalate to a club, or take the wildcard boat-party route.

The wildcard

Start with the boat party.

If you want to go big: do the 18:00 sunset boat party first, then dinner, then Atlantic Bar to carry the night on.

Sunset Boat Party
Wildcard
Vilamoura marina · 18:00–20:00 · €35–45 each
2-hour party along the coast at sunset - live DJ, open bar first hour, dance deck. Departs 18:00, back 20:00. Book well ahead via GetYourGuide.

Atlantic Bar, then see.

The reliable opener. Start here, feel the vibe, and branch out from there.

Atlantic Bar
Base camp
Vilamoura marina · Live music + DJ · Till 02:00

Late escalation

If you're still going: Kadoc.

The Algarve's biggest superclub. Best after 01:00 - not as a first stop.

Kadoc Disco
Late escalation
10-min taxi · Five dance floors · Till 07:00 · ~€12 entry
7,000-capacity superclub, five dance floors, open-air terrace. Mixed-age crowd, less attitude than smaller clubs. Best as a 01:00+ escalation after a couple of marina bars.
