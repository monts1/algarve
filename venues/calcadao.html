Calçadão de Quarteira.

Palm-lined paved promenade between the apartment and Vilamoura marina - flat, easy, scenic. 2km one way. The route you'll use daily without thinking about it.

The Calçadão is the flat, palm-lined paved promenade connecting Quarteira town to Vilamoura marina. You'll use it automatically - it's the route to the marina bars, restaurants, and the Roman ruins. 2km one way, about 15–20 minutes at a relaxed walk.

The beach is directly accessible from the promenade at multiple points - just step off the path and onto the sand. At the Quarteira (western) end, the working fish port and early morning fish market is free to wander through. Authentic and gritty in the best sense.

Good for: evening sunset walks, morning walks before the beach gets busy, and as the default route between the apartment and the marina all week.

Good to know

  • Quarteira fish market is at the eastern end - early morning (before 09:00) is the best time to see it in action
  • Beach access directly off the promenade at multiple points
  • Good for sunset walks - the western light hits the marina end well in the evening
  • The marina end connects directly to Atlantic Bar, Casa do Pescador, and the Roman ruins
