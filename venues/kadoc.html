Kadoc Disco.

The Algarve's biggest superclub. 7,000 capacity, five dance floors, open-air terrace, year-round. Best as a 01:00+ escalation after the marina - not your first stop.

The Algarve's biggest superclub and the correct escalation option. 7,000 capacity across five dance floors with an open-air terrace - the scale is impressive. Year-round operation (unlike Bliss which is summer-only). Mixed-age crowd, less attitude than the smaller clubs.

Go after 01:00 - the real crowd doesn't peak until 02:00 and it runs until 07:00 if you're committed. Entry is around €10–15; a taxi from the marina is about €10. Pre-book tickets online to avoid the queue and usually save a couple of euros.

Five dance floors means you can explore and find the right sound without being stuck. Pace yourselves on the bar - it's a long night if you go early.

Good to know

  • Pre-book tickets online - saves queueing and usually saves €2–3
  • Don't go before midnight - it'll feel dead and you'll burn out early
  • Five floors means explore before settling - find the room with the right sound
  • Mixed-age crowd, less attitude than smaller venues
  • Open till 07:00 - set a sensible exit time or you'll miss Sunday check-out
  • Bring cash for the bar - card machines can be slow in a 7,000-person venue
  • Taxi back to Quarteira from the main road is easier than from inside the car park
