One of Vilamoura's newer venues - stylish Asian-inspired decor with a beach and lounge atmosphere. Both indoor and outdoor zones. Draws a younger and trendier crowd than the established Irish pubs and classic marina bars.

Good cocktails, lounge vibe. A good stop if you want somewhere that feels more like a night out than a holiday bar. Pairs well as a contrast to Atlantic Bar - start at Atlantic for the live music energy, then move here for a more stylish, lower-key drink before deciding whether to escalate to Kadoc.
