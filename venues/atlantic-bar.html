Atlantic Bar.

The reliable opener for Saturday night. Live bands from across Portugal, transitions to DJ later. Marina-side, younger crowd, no aggressive door.

The reliable Saturday night opener. Live music venue first - bands from across Portugal, some UK guests, varying by season. Transitions to live DJ after the band finishes. White baby grand piano in the corner that sets the tone.

Marina-side position means good views and a nice outdoor area. Mixed crowd but skews younger - no aggressive door policy, just show up. Energetic without being a full nightclub, which makes it the right first stop before deciding where the night goes.

From here you can walk to Cats Bar, O'Neills, or MöTAO within minutes. Or stay and let the DJ take over. If the night escalates, Kadoc is a taxi away.
