Sunset Boat Party.

Two-hour boat party along the coast at sunset. Live DJ, open bar first hour, dance deck. Departs Vilamoura marina ~18:00, back ~20:00. Book in advance.

A 2-hour boat party along the Algarve coast at golden hour. Live DJ on board, open bar for the first hour (beer, sangria, soft drinks), dance deck at the top. Solves the "no real club open in late May" question by making the boat the party.

Departs Vilamoura marina around 18:00, returns around 20:00. After that: dinner, then Atlantic Bar to carry the night on. Book through GetYourGuide or SeaBookings at least 2–3 days ahead - these do sell out on Saturdays.

If you go the boat party route, the Saturday itinerary shifts slightly: boat 18:00–20:00, dinner 20:30 (Salmora or similar), Atlantic Bar from 22:00. It makes for a longer, more expensive Saturday but a genuinely different experience.
