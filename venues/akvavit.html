Akvavit.

Iconic Vilamoura marina-front restaurant since 1990. Swedish-Portuguese fusion - grilled tiger prawns, cataplana, lamb chops. Marina-deck terrace. The reliable alternative if your first choice is full.

Vilamoura's most consistent restaurant, open since 1990. Swedish-Portuguese fusion that sounds odd but works: grilled tiger prawns, cataplana, lamb chops - all done properly with marina views from the deck terrace.

This is the swap-in option if Casa do Pescador or Salmora is fully booked. Smart-casual expected. Book at least a day ahead - it's been here 35 years and people know about it. Portuguese wine list is good.
