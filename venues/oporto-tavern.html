Oporto Tavern.

The port answer. Bruno and the team run informal port flights at the bar - they line up 3–5 ports and talk you through each one. Vilamoura marina, open till 02:00.

The port answer for Friday's calm night. Bruno and the team line up 3–5 ports based on what you like - tell them dry, medium or sweet and they'll build a flight around it. Each one gets talked through: variety, age, producer. They pair with tapas cooked on the bar in some sittings.

The weekday happy hour window (17:00–19:30) is the quietest time, when you'll get the most bartender attention. If you want to stay on and have cocktails, they're open till 02:00 - the bar evolves into more of a cocktail lounge later in the evening.

Vibe is informal and sociable rather than educational. Good for people who don't take wine too seriously but want to learn a bit. More relaxed than Rolha a Rolha's structured sit-down format.
