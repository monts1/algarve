O'Neills Irish Pub.

Live music every night, marina-overlooking terrace, pool table inside. Lively without being a nightclub - the simple answer if you want a live band and decent pints.

If the brief is "live band, energetic crowd, decent pints" - this is the simple answer. Live music every night without exception, marina-overlooking terrace with good views, pool table inside if you want to break things up.

Gets crowded after 23:00 and service can slow down. Arrives at the volume where conversation is still possible early on, and more of a shout-in-the-ear situation later. A natural stop on the marina bar-hop - one drink, feel the energy, move on or stay.
