Rolha a Rolha.

A Quarteira wine shop running structured two-hour Portuguese wine tastings. Seven wines walked through by variety and region. Five minutes from the apartment.

The wine alternative to Oporto Tavern. A newer Quarteira wine shop running structured two-hour Portuguese wine tastings - seven wines walked through by variety and region. More formal sit-down vibe than Oporto Tavern's bar-side approach.

The advantage over Oporto Tavern: it's five minutes on foot from the apartment, so you don't need to walk to the marina first. It's a better choice if you want something more educational and structured - they cover different grape varieties across Portugal's wine regions and explain what makes each one distinct.

Book a day or two ahead by phone or DM - they run the tastings in small groups and slots fill up.
