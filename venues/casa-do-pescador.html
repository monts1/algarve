Casa do Pescador.

Traditional Portuguese seafood and steak hidden in a quiet square behind Vilamoura marina. Friday's dinner pick. Famous for cataplana - the copper-pot fish stew for two.

Friday's dinner. Traditional seafood and steak in a hidden square just behind the Vilamoura marina - you need Maps to find it the first time, and it's worth it. Generous portions, serious Portuguese wine list, genuinely traditional cooking without any tourist veneer.

The cataplana is the dish to order - a copper-pot seafood stew designed for two people to share. It takes 20–25 minutes, so order it when you sit down. If seafood isn't your thing, the grilled fish and steak are just as well-reviewed.

Book ahead for Friday - it fills up from about 20:00. Outside tables in the square are lovely if the weather holds.
