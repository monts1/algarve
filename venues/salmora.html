Salmora Live Kitchen.

Saturday's dinner. Modern Portuguese tapas with an open kitchen and social bar seating. Order 6–8 small plates between two - perfect before a big night out on the marina.

Saturday's dinner. Modern Portuguese tapas format with an open kitchen you can see from most seats - the social bar seating is the best spot to grab for two. Order 6–8 small plates between you and share everything.

Signature dishes: mushroom risotto bites, baby squid, gyozas, mini-burgers with house sauce. The food is modern and energetic - not heavy or traditional, which means you leave full but not sleepy, which is exactly right before a big marina night.

Book ahead for Saturday. The cocktail list is worth starting with before the food arrives - they make good opening drinks.
