A little Portuguese

Phrases worth knowing.

European Portuguese pronunciation. Practice obrigado. It'll get you warmer service all week.

Basics & courtesy

Hello / Good morning
Olá / Bom dia
oh-LA / bom DEE-ah
Good afternoon / evening
Boa tarde / Boa noite
BOH-ah TAR-deh / BOH-ah NOY-cheh
Please
Por favor
por fa-VOR
Thank you (m / f speaker)
Obrigado / Obrigada
oh-bree-GA-doo / oh-bree-GA-dah
Cheers!
Saúde!
sah-OO-deh

Cafés (coffee & nata)

One espresso, please
Um café, por favor
oom kaf-EH
Two espressos
Dois cafés, por favor
doysh kaf-EHSH
One nata, please
Um pastel de nata, por favor
oom pash-TEL deh NAH-tah
An espresso AND a nata
Um café e um pastel de nata
oom kaf-EH ee oom pash-TEL
Two of each (coffee + nata)
Dois cafés e dois pastéis de nata
doysh kaf-EHSH ee doysh pash-TAYSH
